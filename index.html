the tale
of knght

In the search for the 3 headed monster bug, Mavis Day surprises even herself with her resourcefulness and skill as a programmer. During her travels, Mavis rescues a harddrive, an heirloom belonging to Tom Hardaway. But when Tom refuses to try designing again, their friendship is strained.

However, Tom is wounded at the Battle of app developing and the two reconcile just before Mavis engages in some serious programming. Tom accepts the harddrive and, with his design prowess, helps win the battle of app developing. The two then returns home as KNGHT.
